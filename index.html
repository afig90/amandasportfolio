ABOUT ME


Hello there! I'm thrilled to share a bit about myself with you. My name is Aamanda Figueroa, and I am a 32-year-old woman on a remarkable journey of self-discovery and growth. At the heart of my life's tapestry is my role as a small business owner. Entrepreneurship has always held a special place in my heart, and I took the leap to start my own venture a few years ago. Navigating the challenges and celebrating the successes of running a small business has been an incredibly rewarding experience, shaping me into the resilient and determined person I am today.

Beyond my professional endeavors, my most cherished role is that of being a mother. I'm blessed to be raising three wonderful daughters, each unique and brimming with potential. Motherhood has been an extraordinary and humbling adventure, teaching me the true meaning of unconditional love, patience, and selflessness. Watching my daughters grow, learn, and blossom brings immense joy to my heart and continually reminds me of the importance of leading by example.

As a military spouse, my life has been marked by its own set of challenges and triumphs. Standing beside my husband, who serves our country with unwavering dedication, has taught me the value of adaptability and resilience. Frequent moves and periods of separation have strengthened our bond and taught me to embrace change as a constant in life.

Now, at this exciting juncture in my journey, I find myself contemplating a change in my career path. While I cherish my role as a small business owner, I am eager to explore new opportunities and discover untapped passions. This desire for change is driven by a hunger for personal growth and a determination to continuously evolve and thrive in all aspects of my life.

As I embark on this new chapter, I am fueled by a thirst for knowledge and a willingness to step outside my comfort zone. With my experiences as a business owner, mother, and military spouse, I possess a unique set of skills, adaptability, and perseverance that I am eager to apply in new arenas.

While the path ahead may be uncertain, I am confident that my entrepreneurial spirit, dedication, and love for my family will guide me towards fulfilling and meaningful endeavors. I am excited to embrace the unknown, embrace change, and uncover the endless possibilities that lie ahead.

Thank you for joining me on this journey of self-discovery and growth. I can't wait to see what the future holds and to make a positive impact in the lives of those around me. Let's take on this adventure together!
